01 · The gap

Enterprise AI has a last-mile problem.

The market is full of slides about AI. We ship the system.

Every enterprise we've worked with had the same story: they bought the platform, hired the consultants, ran the pilot — and then nothing moved to production. The gap was never technology. It was the four operational layers that no platform provides and no advisor wants to own: the analytics to know where to start, the delivery discipline to build at enterprise grade, the commercial engine to capture value instead of competing it away, and the strategy to make the whole thing sustainable.

trIAge120 is a practice built to close that gap. We don't advise. We build the system, we operate it until it works, and we transfer it to the client — with the methodology, the tooling and the discipline built in. In 120 days. That's the BOT model: Build, Operate, Transfer. It's not a framework on a slide. It's a delivery contract.
